Why This Exists

The Problem

DH students arrive ready to do text analysis, build digital archives, and wrangle datasets — but struggle with the basics. Where did my file go? Why won't this path work? What's a terminal? These aren't knowledge gaps; they're invisible barriers that slow everything down.

The Approach

Every guide includes hands-on exercises with immediate feedback, platform-specific instructions for Mac and Windows, real DH project examples, and progressive difficulty from absolute beginner to intermediate. You learn by doing, not by reading documentation.
