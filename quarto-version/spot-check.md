# Quarto Spot-Check List

After rendering Quarto, verify the following:

1) Quizzes
- Answer a couple of quizzes; confirm immediate feedback displays correctly.

2) Terminal Simulator
- Type: `help`, `ls`, `pwd` and confirm output renders.

3) Reading Mode
- Toggle Reading Mode on a guide page.
- Confirm ESC closes it and focus is trapped while open.

4) Exercise Buttons
- Click "Mark Complete"; reload the page and confirm it stays completed.

5) Mobile TOC Toggle
- On a guide page, toggle the TOC with the "Contents" button and verify show/hide works.
